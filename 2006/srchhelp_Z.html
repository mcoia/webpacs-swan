AltaVista-style Truncation
Words may be right-hand truncated using an asterisk. Use a single asterisk * to truncate from 1-5 characters. Use a double asterisk ** for open-ended truncation.
  Examples
  • populat*
  • dost**
AltaVista-style Operators
These do not work the same as Boolean operators; they only apply to one word and they do not modify the relationship between words.Use a + to require a word be present, use a - to exclude a word, if not specified
  Examples
  • nuclear +family (finds any record with nuclear AND family, and any record with family)
  • +nuclear +family (finds any record with nuclear AND family)
  • nuclear -family (finds any record with nuclear AND NOT family, and any record without family)
  • +nuclear -family (finds any record with nuclear AND NOT family)

Type the WORD(S) you want, then click Search
 

Type in Words to search: EXAMPLES
ADJACENCY To search multiple words together as one phrase, enclose in quotes. By default searches will return records containing any of the search terms entered (default OR). "global warming" (finds only records containing the words "global warming" as a phrase)
pollution cancer (finds any record containing "pollution" and any record containing "cancer")
TRUNCATION Words may be right-hand truncated using an asterisk. Use a single asterisk * to truncate from 1-5 characters. Use a double asterisk ** for open-ended truncation. populat*
dost**
OPERATORS These do not work the same as Boolean operators; they only apply to one word and they do not modify the relationship between words. Use a + to require a word be present, use a - to exclude a word, if not specified the assumed operator is OR
  • nuclear +family (finds any record with nuclear AND family, and any record with family)
  • +nuclear +family (finds any record with nuclear AND family)
  • nuclear -family (finds any record with nuclear AND NOT family, and any record without family)
  • +nuclear -family (finds any record with nuclear AND NOT family)

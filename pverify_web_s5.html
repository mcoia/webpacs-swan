Login Help

ID Help

ID Requires 7-digit ID number followed by SBU. Add zeroes at the beginning of the ID until there are 7 digits in the number.

  • Example: 0654321SBU

PIN Help

First-Time Users:

  • Enter a Passcode to Create a Passcode.
  • Passcodes can be a password Number, Password or Passphrase
    • Example:
    • PIN: 12345678
    • Password: MyPassword!
    • Passphrase: ILoveMySBULibrary2014

Returning Users:

  • Enter Your Passcode

Please contact your library if you have any questions.

Login

Students, Staff, and Faculty

Visitors

Your password must be 4 to 30 characters long and should contain a mix of letters and numbers.
New Users

Please type your name, ID followed by your institution code, and click the button "Submit."
You will then be prompted to create your password.

Existing Users

If you forget your password please click "Forget your password?" and enter your name and ID followed by your institution code. Then an email with a link to create a new password will be sent to the email associated with your account.

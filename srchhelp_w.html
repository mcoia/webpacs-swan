Word Search

The catalog will search the library's records, looking for the words that you type in various fields, such as title, contents notes, corporate names, etc.

  • You may use the Boolean operators AND, OR and NOT
  • You may also truncate words with an asterisk *
    For example:
    • Soviet and Russian
    • Soviet Union or Russia
    • Mexico not New
    • (games or toys) and (child* or infant*)
